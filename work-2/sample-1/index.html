Ad Expections

For our first assignment to learn about and get used to photoshop, we were given advertisement examples and told to re-imagine them, whether through a redesign or a parody.
For my first attempt, I simply redesigned it, and cleaned it up to fix the printing erros present in the original image. I redesigned it by simply cutting out unnecessary white space, and bringing the two focuses, the woman and the text, closer together.

For the second attempt, I decided to try a parody. Given the oold style of the dress (as it was an old ad), I thought of making the new advertisement appear to market towards ghosts. I changed the text oval to include "FOR GHOSTS" and some new text, and then removed the plain background to replace it with a spooky mansion. I also isolated the woman's showing skin, and made it more transparent, and her whole body a white shine, to make her appear as a ghost.
