Logo Design

For our fourth and final photoshop design assignment, the class was asked to create or redesign a logo for some brand, whether fictional or nonfictional.
I decided to create a new logo for the University of Pittsburgh, because the current official seal logo doesn't look modern, and I personally don't care for the sports Pitt Panthers logogs. I decided that other than the Panthers, the most recognizable signal of Pitt is of course the Cathedral of Learning, so I decided to use that as the basis for my logos.
For the first try, I simply decided to have the Cathedral in its natural colors, coming out against the sky-blue circle. Next, I decided to recolor the Cathedral in gold, meaning beside the blue it represents Pitt's team colors, and thus represents Pitt even better. Finally, in order to achieve the modern look that I was aiming for, I decided to make the Cathedral more minimalist, adding another color to keep the shadow.
